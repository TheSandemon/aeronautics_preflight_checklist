# ✈️ Aeronautics Preflight Checklist

*A safer launch starts with a better look at your ship.*

**Aeronautics Preflight Checklist** is a client-only utility mod that adds an expandable preflight checklist menu to the **Aeronautics Diagram** item, helping you catch common ship design flaws before takeoff!

Use the Diagram to inspect your contraption and catch design issues before they turn your beautiful flying machine into a falling brick.

---

## ✅ Features

* **Cream Slide-out Sidebar**: A blueprint-style menu that fits perfectly with the native *Create* interface.
* **Scrollable Warnings**: Scissor-clipped scrolling list lets you view all warnings on complex ships without cluttering your screen.
* **Client-Only Utility**: Works on any multiplayer server running *Create: Aeronautics*—no server-side installation required!
* **Magnifying Glass Toggle**: A clean button toggles the preflight checklist panel on and off.
* **Built for Builders**: Keeps the engineering challenge intact. It doesn't fly the ship for you, but tells you what might go wrong before launch.

---

## 🔍 Preflight Diagnostics

The preflight menu scans your ship and checks:

* **⚖️ Mass & Lift**: Displays ship mass in `kpg` (e.g., `23.50 kpg`) and warns you if your engines don't generate enough lift to overcome gravity.
* **📐 Flight Balance**: Checks if the Center of Lift is aligned with the Center of Mass. Warns you of potential drift or tilting if they are offset by more than 0.8 blocks.
* **🚀 Thrust & Torque**: Detects if uneven engine placement will pull the ship sideways or cause it to nose-dive/flip under power.
* **💨 Propeller Clearance**: Alerts you if any propellers or thruster exhausts are blocked by solid blocks.
* **🕹️ Missing Essentials**: Ensures your ship has a pilot seat and a steering helm so it can actually be boarded and steered.
* **📦 Cargo Placement**: Alerts you if cargo containers are placed too high (creating a top-heavy rollover risk) or if cargo weight is lopsided to one side.

* **Multiplayer Servers**: Prevent lost ships and player frustration.
