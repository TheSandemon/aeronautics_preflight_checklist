# ✈️ Aeronautics Preflight Checklist

*A safer launch starts with a better look at your ship.*

**Aeronautics Preflight Checklist** expands the Diagram item from *Create: Aeronautics* with an expandable preflight checklist menu, giving players clearer diagnostics before they send their airship into the sky.

Use the Diagram to inspect your contraption and catch common problems before takeoff — from poor balance and weak lift to blocked propulsion, missing controls, unsafe cargo placement, and other issues that could turn your beautiful flying machine into a falling brick.

---

## ✅ What It Adds

* **Expandable Checklist Panel**: A cream-colored blueprint-style slide-out menu that integrates seamlessly with the *Create* user interface.
* **Scrollable UI**: Features a scissor-clipped viewport and a custom scrollbar, ensuring large checklists on complex ships remain fully readable without cluttering your screen.
* **Quick Preflight Readout**: High-level status markers (Green ✔, Amber ⚠, Red ✖) that let you verify launch readiness at a glance.
* **Builder-Friendly Diagnostics**: Get the exact calculations, offsets, and forces that determine your ship's flight profile.
* **Server-Friendly & Client-Only**: The mod runs entirely on the client side. You can use it on any multiplayer server running *Create: Aeronautics* without needing the mod installed on the server!
* **No Autopilot / Autocrafting**: Does not fly the ship for you or remove the engineering challenge. It simply gives you the info you need to build better.

## 🔍 In-Depth Preflight Checks

The mod performs real-time scans on the ship's sublevel and evaluates the following diagnostics:

### ⚖️ 1. Mass Check
* **What it shows**: Displays the ship's total mass in **kpg** (kilograms per block unit) formatted to the hundredths place (e.g. `23.50 kpg`).
* **Thresholds**: Warns you if the ship's mass is `0.00 kpg` (no blocks found) or flags a critical failure if the total aerodynamic upward lift is insufficient to overcome the ship's gravity. It also provides an amber warning if the safety lift margin is narrow (under `15%`).

### 🎈 2. Lift Check
* **What it shows**: Measures the total upward lift force (in **Newtons (N)**) generated by balloons, wings, and levitation blocks, comparing it directly against gravity.
* **Thresholds**: Alerts you if there are no lift blocks present or reports the exact deficit in Newtons needed to achieve hover/flight.

### 📐 3. Flight Balance
* **What it shows**: Calculates the Center of Lift (CoL) centroid using a force-weighted average:
  $$\mathbf{r}_{CoL} = \frac{\sum \mathbf{r}_i F_i}{\sum F_i}$$
* **Thresholds**: Compares this centroid to the ship's Center of Mass (CoM). If the Center of Lift is offset laterally (left/right) or longitudinally (front/back) by more than **0.8 blocks (meters)**, the mod flags an imbalance and indicates the direction and distance of the deviation.

### 🚀 4. Thrust Check
* **What it shows**: Calculates the total thrust force and net propulsion torque ($\boldsymbol{\tau} = \mathbf{r} \times \mathbf{F}$) about the Center of Mass for all active engines and thrusters.
* **Thresholds**: Flags asymmetric thrust if lateral/yaw torque ($Y$-axis) or vertical/pitch torque ($X$-axis) exceeds **15.0 N·m**. This warns you if uneven engine placement will pull the ship sideways or cause it to nose-dive/flip under power.

### 💨 5. Propeller Clearance
* **What it shows**: Scans the block directly in front of all thrusters, engine bearings, and propellers.
* **Thresholds**: Alerts you if any propulsion block is obstructed by a full solid collision block, preventing clean airflow.

### 🕹️ 6. Missing Essentials
* **What it shows**: Audits the ship's structure for steering helms and boarding seats.
* **Thresholds**: Triggers a critical failure if a pilot seat (e.g., `create:seat`) or a steering wheel (e.g., `simulated:steering_wheel`) is missing, preventing you from piloting or boarding the contraption.

### 📦 7. Cargo Placement Risks
* **What it shows**: Monitors all chests, barrels, vaults, shulker boxes, and crates on board.
* **Thresholds**: Warns you of instability if cargo is placed too high (more than **3.0 blocks** above the Center of Mass, creating a capsizing risk) or if the lateral weight distribution is uneven by more than **4.0 containers** between the left and right sides.

## 🛠️ Built for Builders

This mod **does not fly the ship for you**. It does not replace the physics-based engineering challenge of *Create: Aeronautics*. Instead, it acts as your cockpit diagnostic system, giving you crucial engineering readouts before launch so you can solve design flaws before your ship flips, sinks, stalls, or crashes mid-flight.
